## Purpose

This reading list is a complete, sequenced onboarding canon for new development sessions in the CRE8 repository. It intentionally includes:

- root navigation and governance anchors,
- full `/seed` ethos/provenance corpus,
- full `docs/` SSOT domains,
- machine contracts and evidence templates,
- and session continuity materials used to enter active work safely.

> Interpretation rule: `docs/` remains canonical for normative behavior unless governance explicitly promotes material elsewhere.
