## Quick interpretation
`seed/seed-intro.md` functions as a constitutional preamble for CRE8. Its “attributes” are architectural invariants, security invariants, and governance obligations. The rest of the document set decomposes these into: (a) role/policy rules (`docs/20_*`), (b) data/crypto controls (`docs/40_*`), (c) contract surfaces (`docs/30_*` + `docs/31_*`), (d) feed/content behavior (`docs/50_*`), (e) operational controls (`docs/60_*`), and (f) extension/tracing/governance programs (`docs/70_*`, `docs/80_*`, `docs/00_*`).
